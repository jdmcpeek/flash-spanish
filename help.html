What is Flash-Spanish?

Flash-Spanish is a language learning Chrome extension that lets learners easily create Quizlet flashcards of all the words they look up online.

How does it work?

There are a few ways to use Flash-Spanish:

  1. Through Google Translate. Go to translate.google.com and look up a translation. Then, click the blue "Add to Quizlet" button. Presto! Your word and its translation goes straight to your Flash-Spanish Quizlet set.
  2. Through the Google Translate Chrome extension. Enable the extension, then any word you look up will be added straight to your Quizlet. Try it out!
  3. Through the app! (Coming soon)

How do I begin?

You can start by creating a Quizlet account and authorizing Flash-Spanish to add cards to it. Just click on the Flash-Spanish icon and then the blue "Authorize Quizlet" button. After that, you're ready to go!
